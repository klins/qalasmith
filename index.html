✦ Our Story

Keeping Ancient Crafts Alive

QalaSmith was founded with a simple mission — to bridge the gap between India's incredible artisan communities and customers who value authenticity, quality, and heritage.

We work directly with artisans from Rajasthan, Kerala, Assam, Goa, and across India — ensuring fair prices, ethical sourcing, and preserving techniques passed down through generations.

  • 🤝
    Direct from Artisans No middlemen — your purchase goes directly to the maker.
  • 🌿
    Sustainable Materials Natural dyes, eco-friendly bamboo, and traditional materials.
  • 📦
    Careful Packaging Each item is hand-wrapped in eco-friendly packaging.
  • ✅
    Quality Guaranteed Every product is quality-checked before dispatch.
